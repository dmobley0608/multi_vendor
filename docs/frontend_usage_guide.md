## Vendor Interface

### Viewing Messages
1. Navigate to the **Messages** section.
2. You will see two tabs: **Inbox** and **Outbox**.
3. The **Inbox** tab displays all received messages.
4. The **Outbox** tab displays all sent messages.
5. Click on a message to view its details.

### Sending Messages
1. Click the **New Message** button.
2. Enter the subject and body of the message.
3. Click the **Send** button to send the message.

### Replying to Messages
1. Open a message from the **Inbox**.
2. Click the **Reply** button.
3. Enter the body of the reply message.
4. Click the **Send** button to send the reply.

### Adding Items to Store
1. Navigate to the **Dashboard** section.
2. Click the **Add Item** button.
3. Fill in the item details such as name and price.
4. Click the **Save** button to add the item to your store.

### Updating Store Information
1. Navigate to the **Dashboard** section.
2. Click the **Edit Information** button.
3. Update the necessary fields such as store name, address, and contact information.
4. Click the **Save** button to update your store information.

### Updating Profile
1. Navigate to the **Profile** section.
2. Update your personal information such as name and password.
3. Click the **Save** button to update your profile.

## Staff Interface

### Utilizing the Cash Register
1. Navigate to the **Cash Register** section.
2. Enter the item details.
3. Click the **Add Item** button.
4. Review the items in the cart and click the **Checkout** button.
5. Complete the payment process and print the receipt if necessary.

### Managing Vendors
1. Navigate to the **Vendors** section.
2. You will see a list of all vendors.
3. Click on a vendor to view their details.
4. Click the **Edit** button to update vendor information such as store name, address, and contact information.
5. Click the **Save** button to update the vendor information.

### Generating Reports
1. Navigate to the **Reports** section.
2. Select the type of report you want to generate (e.g., Top Vendors report).
3. Select the date range for the report.
4. Click the **Generate Report** button.
5. View and download the generated report.

### Viewing Messages
1. Navigate to the **Messages** section.
2. You will see two tabs: **Inbox** and **Outbox**.
3. The **Inbox** tab displays all received messages.
4. The **Outbox** tab displays all sent messages.
5. Click on a message to view its details.

### Sending Messages
1. Click the **New Message** button.
2. In the **Select Recipients** field, select the vendors you want to send the message to.
3. Enter the subject and body of the message.
4. Click the **Send** button to send the message.

### Replying to Messages
1. Open a message from the **Inbox**.
2. Click the **Reply** button.
3. Enter the body of the reply message.
4. Click the **Send** button to send the reply.

### Deleting Messages
1. Open a message from the **Inbox** or **Outbox**.
2. Click the **Delete** button.
3. Confirm the deletion in the confirmation dialog.

### Updating Profile
1. Navigate to the **Profile** section.
2. Update your personal information such as name, email, and password.
3. Click the **Save** button to update your profile.
